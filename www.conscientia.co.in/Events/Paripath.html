Circuiter

  1. The event comprises of 2 rounds.
  2. Preliminary round: It will be a written round based on the fundamentals of Electrical, Electronics and application based Integrated circuits.
  3. Finale: A round where you need to design a circuit based on some application. Use your logic to design and solder it on a PCB (Printed Circuit Board) within specified time.
