What is loved more than a comfortable stay after a long day of entertainment! At the hospitality team of Conscientia 2018, we try to make this a reality. We are delighted to have an opportunity to host our participants.

For visitors – ₹50

REACHING THE INSTITUTION

For the ease of the participants, we have listed here the various ways one can reach the institution.
  • From the Central Bus Station, Thampanoor (in front of Thiruvananthapuram Railway Station) take a bus to Ponmudi, Vithura or Valiamala. Alight at ISRO Junction (or IIST directly in case of a Valiamala bus) and follow sign boards to reach IIST campus. Last bus to Vithura from the Central Bus Station is around 9:30 p.m.
  • Alternatively, board a bus from Thampanoor or East Fort Bus Station (around 1 km from Thampanoor) to Nedumangadu. From Nedumangadu, frequent buses are available for ISRO junction. Auto rickshaws are also available from Nedumangadu to IIST (this would cost anywhere between ₹100 - ₹150).
  • It may take a little more than an hour to reach IIST from Thiruvananthapuram city under normal traffic conditions.
  • Bus fare would be around ₹25 per person from Thampanoor to IIST.
  • Prepaid autos and taxis are available from the Central Railway Station (approx ₹300 for an auto and ₹600 for a taxi).
  • Route to be followed: Thampanoor/Central Railway station or East Fort – Kowdiar – Peroorkada – Nedumangad – ISRO Junction – IIST.
  • From the Airport it would be advisable to reach East Fort or Thampanoor and then follow the information given above for buses.
  • Prepaid Taxis are available at the Airport and can be availed. The cost would be anywhere between ₹600 – ₹750.
