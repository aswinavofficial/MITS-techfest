Ricerca


"The progressive development of man is vitally dependent on invention. It is the most important product of his creative brain." - Nikola Tesla.

A platform for the budding researchers, Ricerca promises to test your creativity, coupled with your scientific knowledge and your perseverance. Join in if you feel that you can come up with something original and that you can defend your line of thought with logical explanations.



Departments

  1. Astronomy and Astrophysics
  2. Mechanical
  3. Electrical/Electronics
  4. Computer Science and Information Technology
  5. Physics


Format

  1. The event consists of 2 rounds.
  2. The selection in the first round will be based on the paper submitted.
  3. Every team needs to send their entries to events@conscientia.co.in in Microsoft Word(.doc/.docx) or printable document(.pdf), in IEEE or other internationally recognized formats.
  4. The second round shall consist of a presentation before a bench of judge(s).
  5. The participants will be given 10 minutes to present their paper before a panel of judges and around extra 5 minutes for questions and answers as per discretion of judges.
  6. The team should mention the department under which it chooses to present.

Rules

  1. Only Under-graduates students are eligible for this competition.
  2. A maximum of 2 participants in a team can register.
  3. A team can register for this event in more than one department specified above.
  4. Papers with incorrect format shall be sent back for correction, and can be resubmitted only once, after being returned, failing which they shall be deemed as disqualified.
  5. Exceeding of time limit in the second round will invoke negative points.
  6. For every extra minute taken a certain number of points will be deducted from the total.
  7. The participants can include any type of media (pictures, videos, audio) in their presentation, provided they do not show any controversial clips.
  8. Re-presentation of papers already presented in other competitions / conferences are strictly prohibited. If the team is found to be involved in plagiarism, he/she will be disqualified immediately.
  9. If the candidate fails to meet any one of the above mentioned, he/she will be subjected to disqualification.
  10. Decision of judges will be final and binding on all participants. Any discussions regarding evaluation process at any stage shall not be entertained.
